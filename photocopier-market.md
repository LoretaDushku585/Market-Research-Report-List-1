<p><p>The photocopier market includes several key types: </p><p>1. **Photochemical**: This involves light-sensitive chemicals to create copies, commonly used in older systems. </p><p>2. **Thermal**: This technology uses heat to transfer images onto paper, offering fast and efficient duplication. </p><p>3. **Xerographic**: The most prevalent type, it employs electrostatic charge and toner to produce high-quality copies. </p><p>4. **Digital**: This modern approach uses digital data processing for printing, allowing for versatile and high-resolution output. Each type serves different needs within the market.</p></p>
