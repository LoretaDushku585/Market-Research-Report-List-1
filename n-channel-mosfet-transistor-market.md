<p><p>The N-channel MOSFET transistor market is primarily segmented into enhancement and depletion types. Enhancement-type MOSFETs are normally off devices that require a positive gate-source voltage to conduct, making them widely used in digital circuits and power applications. Conversely, depletion-type MOSFETs are normally on and can be turned off by applying a negative gate voltage, which is beneficial in analog applications where linearity and low resistance are critical. Overall, these types cater to varying demands in electronics and power management.</p></p>
