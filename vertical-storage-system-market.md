<p><p>The vertical storage system market primarily comprises two key types: Vertical Lift Modules (VLM) and Vertical Carousels. VLMs utilize an automated system to store items vertically, allowing for high-density storage and quick retrieval, ideal for managing various inventory types. In contrast, Vertical Carousels consist of rotating shelves that deliver items to an operator, enhancing efficiency in picking processes. Both systems optimize space, improve inventory management, and increase productivity across various industries, including manufacturing, retail, and warehousing.</p></p>
