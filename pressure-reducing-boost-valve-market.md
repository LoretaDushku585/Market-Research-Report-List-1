<p><p>The pressure-reducing (boost) valve market encompasses two primary types: pressure-reducing valves and boost valves. Pressure-reducing valves regulate downstream pressure, ensuring it remains within desired limits to protect equipment and enhance system efficiency. Boost valves, on the other hand, increase pressure within a system, facilitating optimal fluid flow where pressure needs to be raised, such as in irrigation or industrial applications. Both types are crucial for maintaining system integrity and performance in various industries, including water supply and manufacturing.</p></p>
