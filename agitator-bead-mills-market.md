<p><p>Agitator bead mills are vital equipment in the milling industry, classified into dry and wet bead mills. Dry bead mills are designed for processing dry materials and are suitable for applications requiring minimal moisture content. They facilitate particle size reduction and dispersion without using liquids. In contrast, wet bead mills operate with liquids as a medium, allowing for better dispersion and uniformity in particle size, making them ideal for materials that require suspension or emulsion. Both types cater to diverse industrial applications.</p></p>
