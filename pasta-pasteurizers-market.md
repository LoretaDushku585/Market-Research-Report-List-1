<p><ul><li>Compressor Pasteurizer</li><li>Water Circulation Pasteurizer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pasta pasteurizers market features two main types: compressor pasteurizers and water circulation pasteurizers. Compressor pasteurizers utilize refrigeration technology to cool and heat pasta efficiently, ensuring optimal microbial safety. They offer precise temperature control and energy efficiency. In contrast, water circulation pasteurizers use heated water to evenly pasteurize pasta products, maintaining consistency and quality. This method is often preferred for its simplicity and effectiveness in preserving the pasta's texture and flavor while enhancing shelf life.</p></p>
