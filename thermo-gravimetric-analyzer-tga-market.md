The market for TGA is divided into high-pressure TGA and atmospheric TGA. High-pressure TGA operates under elevated pressures, enabling the study of materials in conditions that simulate real-world environments, while atmospheric TGA functions under ambient pressure, providing essential insights into thermal stability and composition for various applications in materials science, pharmaceuticals, and polymers.</p></p>
