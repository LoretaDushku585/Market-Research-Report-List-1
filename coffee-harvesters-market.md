<p><strong>The Coffee Harvesters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vibration Picking Machine (Derricadeira)</li><li>Stripping Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The coffee harvesters market comprises various types, including vibration picking machines (derricadeiras) and stripping machines. Vibration picking machines utilize mechanical vibrations to dislodge coffee cherries from trees, enhancing efficiency and reducing labor costs. In contrast, stripping machines harvest all cherries from branches simultaneously, prioritizing speed over selectivity. Both technologies cater to different farming operations, impacting yield quality and labor requirements, and are integral in modernizing coffee production to meet increasing global demand.</p></p>
