<p><p>The meningitis diagnostic testing market includes various methods to identify pathogens causing meningitis. Latex agglutination tests rapidly detect bacteria using latex beads coated with antibodies. PCR assays offer sensitive genetic material detection for faster diagnosis. Lateral flow assays provide quick, visual results without specialized equipment. Culture tests, though time-consuming, are the gold standard for identifying infectious agents. ELISA tests utilize enzyme-linked antibodies for sensitive detection of specific antigens. Each method has unique advantages, catering to diverse clinical needs.</p></p>
