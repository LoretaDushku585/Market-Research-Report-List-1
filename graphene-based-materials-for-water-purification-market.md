<p><p>Graphene-based materials for water purification include three main types: graphene, graphene oxide, and reduced graphene oxide. Graphene, known for its high conductivity and large surface area, enhances filtration efficiency. Graphene oxide, with its functional oxygen groups, aids in adsorbing contaminants and improving membrane performance. Reduced graphene oxide combines the properties of graphene and graphene oxide, offering better hydrophilicity and facilitating pollutant removal. Collectively, these materials show great promise for developing advanced, effective water purification systems.</p></p>
