<p><p>The Paper Phenolic Copper Clad Laminate market is primarily segmented into two types: single-sided and double-sided copper clad laminates. Single-sided copper clad laminates feature a copper layer on one side, making them ideal for simple circuit designs and lower-cost applications. In contrast, double-sided copper clad laminates have copper layers on both sides, allowing for more complex circuitry and better electrical performance. This versatility in product types caters to various industries, including electronics, telecommunications, and automotive.</p></p>
