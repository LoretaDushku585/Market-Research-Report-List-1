<p><strong>The Plastic Sheet Extrusion Line Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Multilayer</li><li>Single Layer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The plastic sheet extrusion line market is divided into multilayer and single-layer types. Single-layer extrusion involves creating sheets from a single polymer layer, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness, suitable for basic applications. In contrast, multilayer extrusion combines multiple layers of different polymers, enhancing properties such as strength, barrier, and aesthetic appeal, making these sheets ideal for specialized applications in packaging and construction. The choice between these types depends on the specific functional requirements and end-use applications.</p></p>
