<p><p>The Automotive Camera Monitor System (CMS) enhances vehicle safety by replacing traditional mirrors with camera displays. The market comprises two main types: Wireless Type and Wired Type. Wireless CMS uses Bluetooth or Wi-Fi technology to transmit video feeds from cameras to monitors, offering easier installation and reduced wiring complexity. In contrast, Wired Type systems utilize physical connections for data transfer, ensuring stable, high-quality images and lower latency. Each type caters to different consumer preferences, installation environments, and cost considerations.</p></p>
