<p><p>Clinical Trials Site Management Organizations (SMOs) operate across various phases of clinical trials, each serving specific needs. Phase I focuses on safety and dosage in a small group, typically healthy volunteers. Phase II evaluates efficacy and side effects in a larger patient population. Phase III assesses the drug's effectiveness in larger, diverse populations, crucial for regulatory approval. Phase IV, or post-marketing studies, monitor long-term effects and gather additional data after a drug's approval, ensuring ongoing safety and efficacy.</p></p>
