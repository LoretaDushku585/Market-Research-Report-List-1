<p><p>The surface mounting adhesives market is categorized into non-conductive and conductive adhesives. Non-conductive adhesives are primarily used to bond electronic components without affecting electrical performance, providing reliable insulation and mechanical stability. In contrast, conductive adhesives contain metallic particles, facilitating electrical connections between components, crucial for applications requiring circuit functionality. Both types are essential in electronics manufacturing, catering to diverse needs in assembling circuit boards, ensuring quality, durability, and performance in various electronic devices.</p></p>
