<p><p>The liquid-based needle-free injector market consists of several types, each utilizing different mechanisms. Jet injectors use high-pressure jets to deliver medication through the skin without needles, ideal for rapid administration. Spring-based injectors operate through mechanical springs that propel the liquid, ensuring precise dosing. Laser injectors utilize focused energy to create micro-holes in the skin, allowing for drug absorption. Vibration injectors employ ultrasonic vibrations to facilitate medication delivery, enhancing comfort and minimizing pain, making them suitable for various therapeutic applications.</p></p>
