<p><strong>The Vehicle Speed Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hall Type</li><li>Magnetic Electric Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Vehicle Speed Sensor (VSS) market comprises various types, primarily Hall Effect and Magnetic Electric sensors. Hall Effect sensors utilize a magnetic field to detect speed, offering high precision and reliability, making them ideal for modern vehicles. Meanwhile, Magnetic Electric sensors operate by generating voltage signals in response to magnetic variations from gears or wheels, providing a cost-effective solution. Both types are essential for accurate speed measurement, contributing to improved vehicle performance and safety in automotive applications.</p></p>
