<p><strong>The Sweet Whey Powder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fully Demineralized</li><li>Incomplete Demineralized</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sweet whey powder market is categorized into two main types: fully demineralized and incompletely demineralized. Fully demineralized sweet whey powder has a low mineral content, making it suitable for applications needing high purity, such as infant formula and specialized dietary products. In contrast, incompletely demineralized sweet whey powder retains some minerals, providing nutritional benefits and flavor, making it ideal for cheese production, bakery products, and nutritional supplements. Both types serve distinct roles in food and nutrition industries.</p></p>
