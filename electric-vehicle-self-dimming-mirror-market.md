<p><p>The Electric Vehicle Self-Dimming Mirror market comprises two main types: outer self-dimming mirrors and inside self-dimming mirrors. Outer self-dimming mirrors are installed on the vehicle's exterior, automatically adjusting their tint to reduce glare from headlights of trailing vehicles, enhancing nighttime driving safety. Inside self-dimming mirrors, typically located in the vehicle's cabin, serve a similar purpose by minimizing glare from behind, improving visibility for the driver. Both types enhance comfort and safety, driving demand in the electric vehicle market.</p></p>
