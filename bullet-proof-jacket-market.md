<p><p>The bulletproof jacket market can be categorized into three main types: soft vests, hard vests, and composite vests. Soft vests are lightweight and flexible, typically made from materials like Kevlar, designed for comfort and ease of movement, mainly used by law enforcement. Hard vests feature rigid plates that provide enhanced protection against high-caliber firearms and are often utilized by military personnel. Composite vests combine elements of both, offering a balance of flexibility and protection, tailored for varied tactical needs.</p></p>
