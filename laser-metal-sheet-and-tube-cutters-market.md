<p><p>Laser metal sheet and tube cutters are categorized into two main types: Open Type and Full Protection Type. Open Type cutters feature a minimalist design that allows for easy access to the workpiece, facilitating quick adjustments and maintenance. However, this design offers less safety, exposing operators to potentially hazardous elements. In contrast, Full Protection Type cutters provide comprehensive shielding, enhancing operator safety and reducing the risk of accidents while maintaining precision and efficiency in cutting operations.</p></p>
