<p><p>MEMS fiber optic switches are crucial components in optical networks, facilitating signal routing. The market is divided into two main types: single-mode fiber optic switches and multimode fiber optic switches. Single-mode switches are designed for long-distance communication, utilizing a single light path to enable high bandwidth and low signal loss. In contrast, multimode switches use multiple light paths, suitable for shorter distances and often employed in local area networks, providing cost-effectiveness but with higher signal dispersion.</p></p>
